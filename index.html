NeoPark

Smarter Parking Starts Here

About the Project

Hi, I'm Anshu Arunav from Grade 6. I built NeoPark — an automatic parking gate that opens and closes using sensors and an RFID card. I was inspired by the problems I saw at real parking lots and wanted to create something better, safer, and smarter!

NeoPark Features

NeoPark is designed to manage parking spaces efficiently without the need for on-site staff. It ensures secure access, real-time slot tracking, and smooth vehicle flow.

How NeoPark Works

  • IR Sensor 1 detects the car at the gate.
  • The LCD shows "Scan your card."
  • If the RFID card is valid, the gate opens.
  • The car passes through IR Sensor 2, and the gate closes after 2 seconds.
  • The display updates the available slots in real time.

Parts Used

Arduino Uno
IR Sensors
RFID Reader + Card
Servo Motor
LCD Display
Wires + Breadboard

What I Learned

This project was not easy! I had to fix bugs in the code, make sure the sensors were placed correctly, and even solve power issues. But I never gave up. I learned about electronics, coding, and how real engineers solve problems.

What’s Next?

In the future, I want to upgrade NeoPark by adding a mobile app, solar power, and even license plate detection. Maybe one day, it can be used in real malls or offices!
